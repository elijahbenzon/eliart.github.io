About Me

Elijah Benzon, also known as Eli, strives to explore all different media of art. Most of his early work consisted mostly of two-dimensional art, especially graphite drawings. When he started to take art classes in college, Elijah experimented with three-dimensional art, using materials such as wood, metal, plaster, and glass and with electronic media including Illustrator and Photoshop. In college, Elijah intertwined personal struggles and societal concerns into his artwork. In the future, Elijah hopes to further define his artist statement and to use his artwork to inspire others.

Born and raised in Fort Myers, Florida, Elijah is currently a fourth-year undergraduate student in the University of Miami. In Miami, he studies biology, psychology, and art and participates in two student-led organizations: the Canes Emergency Response Team and the Locked Dance Team. Elijah loves incorporating his travel experiences and interest in different cultures in his artwork. He has traveled to many countries, including Canada, Peru, the Bahamas, the Philippines, South Korea, and Thailand.
