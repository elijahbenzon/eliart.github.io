Eli's Art

Welcome to Eli's Art

You are visiting the portfolio and blog of Eli, that's me! I am an independent artist and recent college graduate looking to further my studies into medical illustration. Most of my artwork consists of realistic depictions, especially portraits, the human figure, and still lifes, but I sometimes experiment with abstract and contemporary designs. I mainly create graphite drawings, but I also enjoy painting, sculpture, and electronic media.
