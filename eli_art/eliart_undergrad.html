Undergraduate Work

During his years in university, Eli stepped out of his comfort zones of two-dimensional graphite drawings and branched out into different forms of art including sculpture, plaster, wood, metal, electronic media, glass blowing, and printmaking. Here, you will find the pieces he has made. Due to the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic, not all pieces have been completed, but Eli cherishes the skills he has experienced and practiced.
