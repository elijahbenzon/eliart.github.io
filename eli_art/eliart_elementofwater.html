Element of Water

Water is perhaps the most common thing known to humans, but despite its commonplace, it possesses the most wonderful and miraculous properties. It has the ability to change form whether an energetic gas, a maleable liquid, or a hard solid. Water can heal yet destroy. Every living things needs it to survive, but too much of it can kill. In this series, Eli shows this diverse spectrum of abilities the element of water embodies.
