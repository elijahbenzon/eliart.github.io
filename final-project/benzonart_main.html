Introduction

Elijah Benzon, also known as Eli, strives to explore all different media of art to voice personal issues and societal concerns. Inspired by his last few semesters in university and the impact of COVID-19, Elijah has started a new series of artwork labeled "Identity Crisis", where he explores the events and thoughts he experienced during these times. He hopes his work will inspire others to embrace their true selves and make others aware of the current issues with society.

Additionally, through his artwork, Eli wants to show the evolution of style, his inspirations for his work, and how his style has changed through time and personal growth. He believes origins are important to understand an individual as a whole, and he hopes you will find inspiration to create regardless of skill or material.
