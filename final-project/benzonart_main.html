Benzon Art

Art can permeate the very deepest part of us, where no words exist.

This quote stated by Eileen Miller is why I make art. As any artist would say, art provided me a way to express my feelings, my struggles, and my thoughts on the world around us. However, art also acted as a weapon to challenge believes and offer new perspectives. In my artwork, I try to combine concepts from psychology, ranging from emotions to social, with art. This is best represented through my newest series Identity Crisis, which I challenge the perception of identity, using concepts from Erik Erikson, Jean Piaget, Lawrence Kohlberg, and Alfred Kinsey.

When you explore my artwork, I encourage you to view these pieces with an open-mind and look beyond what the artwork physically displays. The artwork I currently have will continue to grow as I further define myself as an artist. As a budding artist, I hope to further my perspectives by opening to collaborations with other artists.
