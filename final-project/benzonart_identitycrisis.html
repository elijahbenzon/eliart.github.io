Identity Crisis

The concept of identity has been studied and redefined throughout time. According to Erik Erikson's theory of psychosocial development, adolescents undergo the stage of identity vs. role confusion, where search for a sense of self and personal identity, through exploration of values, beliefs, and goals. When individuals deal with role confusion, or also known as identity crisis, they begin to question their identity and try different lifestyles. This path can either lead to fulfillment of their identity or unhappiness, known as the negative identity. This process can extend into adulthood. In this series of artwork, Elijah explores the identity crises many individuals, including himself, undergo. He touches on concpets including gender roles, culture, sexual orientation, family dynamics, and mental health.
