Latest Work

One core characteristic Elijah strives to embody is to always be curious and explore new things. Art is currently and always has been evolving, incorporating technology, new ideas, and new medias. During his years in university, Elijah stepped out of his comfort zones of two-dimensional graphite drawings and branched out into different forms of art including sculpture, plaster, wood, metal, electronic media, glass blowing, and printmaking. Here you will find the recent pieces he has made.
