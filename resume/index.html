About Me

Elijah, also known by Eli, is currently a senior at the University of Miami, majoring in biology and psychology and minoring in chemistry and art. He is proudly part of two organizations on campus: the Locked Dance Team and the Canes Emergency Response Team. The Locked Dance Team is student-run dance team that performs various k-pop dances on campus and posts covers on social media. The Canes Emergency Response Team is a student-run organization that teach members emergency preparedness, triage, and basic first aid. Elijah loves to travel and explore new cultures and cuisines. He has been to countries such as the Philippines, South Korea, Thailand, the Bahamas, Mexico, and Peru. In his free time, Elijah loves to sketch, play video games, swim, and hang out with friends.
